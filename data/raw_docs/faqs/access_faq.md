# Access FAQs

## How do I get access to internal systems?
Access is usually granted after onboarding is completed and your manager submits an access request. Most access is provisioned automatically.

## How long does it take to get access?
In most cases, access is available within one business day. Some systems may take longer depending on approval requirements.

## Who approves access requests?
Access requests are typically approved by your manager or the system owner.

## Can contractors or interns get access?
Yes, contractors and interns can be granted access if required for their role.

## Why can’t I access a system I need?
Access may be restricted due to security controls, incomplete onboarding, or missing approvals.

## What should I do if my access stops working?
If your access stops working, contact the support team to restore it.

## Can I request access on behalf of someone else?
Managers may request access for their team members.

## What happens to my access when I change roles?
Access is updated automatically when roles change.

## What happens to my access when I leave the company?
Access is removed as part of the offboarding process.
