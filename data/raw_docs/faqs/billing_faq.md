# Billing & Refund FAQs

## How am I billed for my subscription?
Billing is handled automatically based on your selected plan and billing cycle.

## When will I be charged?
You are charged at the start of each billing period. Renewal charges happen automatically unless cancelled.

## Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

## Will I get a refund if I cancel?
In most cases, eligible refunds are processed according to our refund policy.

## How long does it take to receive a refund?
Refunds are typically processed within a few business days after approval.

## Can I get a refund for a renewal charge?
Renewal refunds may be available depending on timing and usage.

## What happens if a payment fails?
If a payment fails, we will retry automatically and notify you to update your payment method.

## Can I change my billing plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes usually take effect immediately.

## Who should I contact for billing issues?
For billing-related questions or issues, contact the support team.
