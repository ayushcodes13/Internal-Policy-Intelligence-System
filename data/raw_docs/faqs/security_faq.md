# Security FAQs

## How is my data protected?
We use industry-standard security practices to protect customer data, including access controls, monitoring, and encryption where appropriate.

## Is my data encrypted?
Sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest using accepted encryption standards.

## Who has access to customer data?
Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel who require it to perform their job functions.

## Do you monitor for security incidents?
Yes, systems are monitored for suspicious activity and potential security incidents.

## What happens if there is a security incident?
If a security incident is detected, it is handled according to internal incident response procedures to minimize impact and restore normal operations.

## Do you perform security audits or reviews?
Security controls and processes are reviewed periodically to maintain an appropriate security posture.

## Is customer data shared with third parties?
Customer data is not shared with third parties except where required to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

## How can I report a security concern?
Security concerns should be reported through the designated support or security contact channels.
