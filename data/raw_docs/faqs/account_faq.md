# Account & Access FAQs

## How do I create an account?
Accounts can be created through the official signup process on the website.

## How do I reset my password?
Password reset options are available through the login page using your registered email address.

## Can I change my account email or details?
Certain account details can be updated from the account settings page.

## How do I close my account?
Account closure requests can be submitted through customer support or account management settings, where available.

## What happens to my data if I close my account?
Data handling after account closure follows internal data retention and deletion policies.

## Can multiple users access the same account?
Account access rules depend on the plan and configuration associated with your account.

## What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?
If you suspect unauthorized access, contact customer support immediately and reset your credentials.

## Is account activity logged?
Account activity may be logged for security, auditing, and troubleshooting purposes.
