# Support & SLA FAQs

## How can I contact customer support?
You can contact customer support through the official support channels provided in your account or on the website.

## What are your support hours?
Support is available during standard business hours, with additional coverage for critical issues.

## Do you offer 24/7 support?
24/7 support may be available for certain plans or for high-priority incidents.

## How quickly will my issue be addressed?
Response times depend on the nature and priority of the issue. Critical issues are addressed faster than general inquiries.

## What is considered a critical issue?
A critical issue typically involves a service outage or a severe impact on core functionality.

## Do you guarantee response or resolution times?
Target response and resolution times are defined in our Service Level Agreement (SLA). These targets may vary based on issue priority and plan type.

## How do I report a service outage?
Service outages should be reported immediately through the designated support channels.

## Will I receive updates on my support ticket?
Yes, updates are provided through the support system as your ticket progresses.

## Can issues be escalated?
Yes, unresolved or high-impact issues may be escalated according to internal escalation procedures.

## Where can I find more details about support commitments?
Detailed support commitments and service levels are outlined in the SLA and related support documentation.
