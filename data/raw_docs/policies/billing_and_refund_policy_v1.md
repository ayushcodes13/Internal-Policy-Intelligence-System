# Billing and Refund Policy (v1)

## Scope
This policy defines cancellation and refund eligibility for subscription-based products.

## Subscription Cancellation
Upon cancellation of a subscription, users must discontinue use of the service and remove any installed software or access credentials associated with the subscription.

## Refund Eligibility

### Annual Subscriptions
- Annual subscriptions are eligible for a **full refund if requested within 60 days** of the purchase date.
- Annual renewal charges are also eligible for a full refund if requested within **60 days of renewal**.
- Refunds are not applicable if services have already been redeemed.

### Monthly Subscriptions
- Monthly subscriptions are eligible for a **full refund if requested within 14 days** of the initial purchase.
- Subsequent monthly renewals are **not eligible** for refunds.

### Trial Subscriptions
- If a trial converts into a paid subscription, the first charge is eligible for a refund:
  - Within **60 days** for annual plans
  - Within **14 days** for monthly plans
- Subsequent renewals are not eligible for refunds.

### Add-On Subscriptions
- Cancelling a primary subscription automatically cancels all linked add-on subscriptions.
- Add-on subscriptions may also be cancelled independently.
- Refund eligibility for add-ons follows the same rules as the primary subscription.

## Refund Processing
- Refund requests must be submitted through customer support.
- Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method.
- Processing may take several business days.

## Auto-Renewal
- Users may disable auto-renewal prior to the billing date.
- Disabling auto-renewal does not cancel the active subscription term.
