# Account Termination and Deletion Policy

## Scope
This policy defines the conditions and procedures for voluntary and involuntary account termination.

## Voluntary Account Deletion
Users may request permanent deletion of their account through the support team. Requests must be verified to prevent unauthorized deletions.

### Verification
The company may require identity verification, including email confirmation, security questions, or other verification mechanisms.

### Processing Timeline
Verified deletion requests are processed within 10–15 business days.

## Data Deletion and Retention
Upon completion:
- Accounts are permanently disabled
- Personal data is removed from active systems
- Backup data may be retained temporarily in accordance with retention policies
- Deletion is irreversible

## Involuntary Account Termination
Accounts may be suspended or permanently terminated in cases of:
- Repeated policy violations
- Posting obscene or prohibited content
- Abuse of platform services

Termination may occur without user-initiated deletion requests.

## Legal and Compliance Exceptions
Certain data may be retained as required by applicable laws or regulatory obligations.
