# Cancellation and Refund Policy (v2)

## Scope
This policy governs cancellation and refund eligibility for all subscription plans and supersedes previous refund policies.

Refunds will not be issued if account termination occurs due to violations of the Terms of Service.

## Refund Period
Users may cancel their subscription at any time. However, refunds are permitted **only during the Refund Period**, defined as follows:

- If the subscription purchase was assisted by an authorized sales agent:  
  Refund requests must be made within **7 days of the account setup appointment**.
- For all other purchases:  
  Refund requests must be made within **7 days of the purchase date**.

## Refund Restrictions
- Each user is eligible for **only one refund**.
- Refunds are **not applicable** to:
  - Subscription renewals
  - Subscription upgrades (unless purchased within the Refund Period)
- Refund requests must be submitted **only through official customer support channels**.

Requests submitted via email, chat, or informal communication channels are not eligible.

## Refund Processing
- Eligible refunds are processed within **20 working days**, subject to processing timelines of payment service providers.
- The company may deduct transaction fees, bank charges, or processing costs.
- Users must provide accurate payment and banking details to facilitate refunds.

## Exclusions
Refunds are not available for subscriptions:
- Purchased using referral programs
- Purchased via third-party no-cost EMI or financing options

## Legal Override
The company may retain certain data or deny refunds as required by applicable laws or regulatory obligations.
