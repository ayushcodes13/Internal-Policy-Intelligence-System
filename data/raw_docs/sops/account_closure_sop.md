# Account Closure and Data Erasure SOP

## Purpose
This SOP defines the procedure for handling account closure and personal data erasure requests.

It applies to requests made under applicable data protection regulations.

---

## Right to Erasure
Individuals may request erasure of their personal data where one or more of the following applies:
- Data is no longer required for its original purpose
- Consent for processing has been withdrawn
- Processing is unlawful
- Erasure is required to meet legal obligations
- The data subject was a minor at the time of collection

---

## Verification
- Identity verification is mandatory before processing erasure requests.
- Requests made by agents require documented authorization.

---

## Erasure Process
- Eligible data will be removed from active systems without undue delay.
- Account access will be permanently disabled.
- Deletion is irreversible once completed.

---

## Exceptions
Data may not be erased where:
- Retention is required for legal or regulatory compliance
- Data is required for legal claims or dispute resolution
- Data exists only in immutable or technical backups

Backups may be retained temporarily in accordance with retention policies.

---

## Communication
- Requesters will be informed of the outcome of their request.
- Where erasure is denied or limited, the reason must be documented.

---

## Escalation
Disputes or complex cases must be escalated to legal or data protection authorities.
